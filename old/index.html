Aryaman Reddi

What is intelligence and how do we generate it?

I am a PhD student in reinforcement learning at the LiteRL group and Intelligent Autonomous Systems lab at TU Darmstadt, supervised by Professor Carlo D'Eramo.

I am interested in developing sample-efficient techniques in deep reinforcement learning using theoretical analyses from game theory.

I received a BA and MEng in Information Engineering at Homerton College, University of Cambridge, where I was awarded the David Thompson prize for high achievement. Before my PhD, I worked at Arm as a machine learning research engineer.

I am a Cartesian , a Continuum Hypothesis supporter, and a Collatz conjecture sceptic.
